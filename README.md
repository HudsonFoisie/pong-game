# Pong Game with Battle Pass System

Welcome to the Pong Game! This is a classic pong game with a twist: a battle pass system that keeps the gameplay engaging and rewarding.

## Game Features
- **Battle Pass System:** Progress through levels and unlock rewards as you play.
- **Controls:**
  - Left Paddle: **W** (up) and **S** (down)
  - Right Paddle: **Arrow Keys** (up and down)
- **Audio Effects:** Enjoy authentic gameplay with Kurt Cobain moan sounds that play on ball hits.

## How to Play
1. Use the controls to move your paddles and hit the ball.
2. Score points by getting the ball past your opponent's paddle.
3. Complete challenges to progress through the battle pass and unlock unique rewards!
